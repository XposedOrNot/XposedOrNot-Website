Understanding Data Breach Visualization

This interactive visualization displays major data breaches organized by year, providing insights into the scope and impact of cybersecurity incidents over time.

How to use this visualization:

  • Circle Size: Represents the number of records exposed in each breach
  • Color Scheme: Colors are grouped by year to help identify trends
  • Logos: Company/organization identifiers appear in the center of each circle
  • Hover: Move your cursor over any breach to see detailed information

The visualization automatically arranges breaches to minimize overlap while keeping them organized by year.
