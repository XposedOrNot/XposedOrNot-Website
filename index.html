In today's digital age, your emails open doors to both your personal and professional worlds.

Are they securely locked?

Picture this: a business owner stumbles upon their company email being auctioned online, while a college student is shocked to discover her personal email associated with an unknown data breach.

Both blindsided. Both vulnerable. This scenario isn't limited to them—it could very well be you next.

XposedOrNot is your digital shield. Our intuitive, open-source tool stands ready to inform you about the safety of your email. Should there be any issues, we ensure you're the first to know, allowing swift action on your part.

Transparency is our hallmark. Dive into our operations, and if inspired, contribute. We actively welcome contributions to both our code and breach data from individuals and organizations around the globe.
